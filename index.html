Our Heritage

Where Local Tradition Meets Modern Passion

Established in the vibrant hub of Dukem, Dako Cafe is built around a love for premium coffee culture. Every morning, our team select-roasts raw Arabica beans sourced directly from certified organic farms in Yirgacheffe and Sidamo, creating a velvety macchiato and dense espresso that have become neighborhood staples.

"We set out to create a community living room where locals, visiting business professionals, and families can gather over outstanding coffee and gourmet bistro dishes prepared from scratch daily."

— Dako Cafe Founding Team
